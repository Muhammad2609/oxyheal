Hyperbaric Oxygen Therapy: Oxyheal Ltd is a London-based hyperbaric chamber specializing in hyperbaric oxygen therapy. We offer a range of hyperbaric oxygen treatments to support your health and wellbeing.

Oxygen Therapy Services: Oxyheal Ltd provides Hyperbaric Oxygen Therapy treatments in London for a range of conditions including sports injuries, to reduce pain, improve cognitive function, and more.

What is Hyperbaric Oxygen Therapy? HBOT involves breathing pure oxygen in a pressurized chamber, allowing your body to absorb higher levels of oxygen than usual. This increased oxygen promotes natural healing processes and can have a profound impact on various health conditions.

Benefits of HBOT:

  • Increased oxygen levels in the blood
  • Boosted immune system
  • Promoted tissue repair
  • Reduced inflammation
  • Improved wound healing
  • Reduced pain
  • Improved cognitive function
  • Enhanced sports performance
